the command i used to look at the first 10 lines of a file was
head -10 filename eg head -10 top-ten-songs.html
the command i used to look at the last 10 lines of a file was
tail -10 filename eg tail -10 top-ten-songs.html

grep -r 'text' path eg grep -r 'The Nightingale' 2015-books/ (from the 2015-media folder)
