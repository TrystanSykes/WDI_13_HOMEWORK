The conclusion to the riftwar saga A Darkness at Sethanon brings to a head the confrontation between the resurgent leader of the Moredhel Murmandamus and the prophesized 'Lord of the West', Prince Arutha. Murmandamus bring the combined might of his people crashing down through the Teeth of the World seeking to take the Kingdom city of Sethanon and unlock the ancient secret lying below its streets.
